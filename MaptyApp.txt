MAPTY APP

1. Click on the map to set the location of your workout.
2. Choose in the form that appears on the sidebar if your workout is running or cycling.
3. Fill the form with the distance (km), the duration (min) and the cadence (step/min) for running or the elevation gain (meters) for cycling.
4. Keep track of the type, the place, the date and the information of your workouts.
